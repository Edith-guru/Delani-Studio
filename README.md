# Delani Studio


### A portfolio showcasing software services that Delani studio offers to its customers.
#### By **Edith Nekesa**

## Description
Delani studio is a website that is dedicated to connecting you with  developers to build your website. Through the website, you can get to know who they are and what they do too. The site also give you a chance to take a sneak peek at their past projects and contact for enquiry and more. The video below is a snippet of how the website works.

![Delani Studio (4)](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/32816069/158372434-0c460050-9cb8-4ba5-8d68-ecd2dafb3690.gif)

## Sections
* **About us**
Get to know who we are and what our values are.
* **Services**
Here, we give you the chance to interact with the services we offer and how we go through a project so as to deliver to you your final and finished product.
* **What we do**
This contains our three main activities which are design, development, and product management.
* **Portfolio**
This is just a brief overview of past projects we have done.
* **Contact us**
You are free to contact us and we will make sure we get back to you.
